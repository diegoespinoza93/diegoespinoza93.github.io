I am a PhD candidate in Medieval Studies at the Centre for Medieval Studies of the University of Toronto.

My research traces the medieval antecedents of questions concerning rationality and rational belief. I am particularly interested in the epistemic and moral dimensions of testimonial deference and how rational opinions should be constrained by our evidence.

Welcome to my academic website. Here you can find information about my research, teaching, and publications.
